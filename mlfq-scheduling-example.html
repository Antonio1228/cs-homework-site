MLFQ Scheduling Example

This is an exam-style, step-by-step worked example of Multi-Level Feedback Queue (MLFQ) scheduling. We use two levels: - Queue 1: Round Robin with quantum = 4 - Queue 2: FCFS (processes demoted from Q1 run to completion)

Scheduling Rules

Rules applied in this example (typical simple MLFQ policy):

  1. All new processes enter Queue 1 (RR, quantum = 4).
  2. If a process uses its entire quantum in Queue 1 without finishing, it is demoted to Queue 2.
  3. Queue 2 is FCFS and runs processes to completion (no further demotion).
  4. At any time, Queue 1 has priority over Queue 2. Only when Queue 1 is empty do we schedule Queue 2 processes.
